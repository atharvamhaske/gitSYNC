# Privacy Policy for GitSync

**Last updated:** January 2, 2026

## Overview

GitSync is a Chrome extension that helps you sync your LeetCode solutions to GitHub. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

### What we collect:
- **GitHub Access Token**: Stored locally in your browser to authenticate with GitHub API
- **Repository URL**: Stored locally to know where to push your solutions

### What we DON'T collect:
- We do NOT collect any personal information
- We do NOT track your browsing activity
- We do NOT sell or share any data with third parties
- We do NOT store any data on external servers (except GitHub for syncing)

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. Your GitHub token never leaves your device except when communicating directly with GitHub's API.

## Third-Party Services

- **GitHub API**: Used to push your solutions to your repository. Subject to [GitHub's Privacy Policy](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement)
- **LeetCode**: We read problem information from LeetCode pages you visit. Subject to [LeetCode's Privacy Policy](https://leetcode.com/privacy/)

## Data Deletion

You can delete all stored data by:
1. Removing the extension from Chrome
2. Or clearing the extension's storage in Chrome settings

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

## Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
